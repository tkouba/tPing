Usage: tping target_name -p port [-t] [-a] [-n count] [-l size] [-f] [-i TTL] [-v TOS]
             [-r count] [-s count] [[-j host-list] | [-k host-list]]
             [-w timeout] [-R] [-S srcaddr] [-c compartment] [-p]
             [-4] [-6] 

Options:
    -t             Attempt to connect to the specified host until stopped.
                   To stop - press Control-C or Control-Break.
    -a             Resolve address to hostname.
    -n count       Number of attempts to connect.
    -w timeout     Timeout in milliseconds to wait for each reply.
    -p port        Connect to port.
    -4             Force using IPv4.
    -6             Force using IPv6.
